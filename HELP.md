## 🚀 Quick Start Checklist

* **Launch the App**: Run `Start_TomeMaster.bat` in the root folder.
* **Select Your Consulting Brain**:
  * Open **Settings (Gear Icon)**.
  * Connect your API Key (Gemini, Claude, or OpenAI).
  * **Pro Tip**: Use *Gemini 3.1 Pro* for full-novel structural audits.
* **Import Your Draft**:
  * Support for `.docx`, `.txt`, and `.pdf`.
  * Use **Vision OCR** for handwritten journals or scanned manuscripts.

## 🏛️ The AI Boardroom

The "Boardroom" consists of specialized AI Personas that critique your work from different professional angles:

* **Developmental Editor**: Scans your overall story rhythm and suggests chapter breaks.
* **Copy Editor**: Scans for grammar and tone, providing interactive "squiggles" to accept or ignore.
* **Sensitivity Reader**: Checks for demographic accuracy and cultural tropes.
* **Marketing Executive**: Generates your back-cover blurb and pitch.
* **Hollywood Screenwriter**: Scans for script adaptation potential.

## 🎙️ Voice Commands

Always begin commands with **"Computer"** or **"System"**:

* *"Computer, Run Analysis"* — Triggers agent feedback.
* *"Computer, Magic Wand"* — Refreshes Table of Contents.
* *"Computer, Export PDF"* — Instantly generates your press-ready PDF.

## 🔒 Privacy & Data Sovereignty

Tome-Master is built on the principle of **Total Data Sovereignty**:

* **Strictly Local**: Your work is saved to a local **IndexedDB** vault, never a central database.
* **No Cloud Residency**: When using cloud AI, your text is processed **in-memory** and purged instantly.
* **Offline Mode**: Toggle the status bar to "Local Only" and use **Ollama** for 100% air-gapped writing.
